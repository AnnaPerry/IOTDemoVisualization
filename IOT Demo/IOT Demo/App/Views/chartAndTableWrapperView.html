Welcome!

The bar chart that you see now is driven by sensor data collected from your own mobile device! Try shaking your device to generate new X, Y, and Z accelerometer data--or try generating new light level and proximity data by covering up the front of the phone with your hand. That new sensor data will be written to the gateway PI Server and then visualized here on this chart!
