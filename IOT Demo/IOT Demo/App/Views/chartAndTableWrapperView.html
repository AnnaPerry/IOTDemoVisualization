The bar chart is driven by sensor data collected from your own smart phone! Try shaking your device to generate new X, Y, and Z accelerometer data! Try covering up your phone screen to see the light level and proximity level drop, and uncover the phone to see the light and proximity bars grow again. Rotate your phone to see the alpha, beta, and gamma values change on the plot, and shake your phone to see the X, Y, and Z axis motion increase!
